About Us

Treeranga is committed to transforming the world of fashion by offering high-quality, eco-friendly t-shirts. Our mission is to provide customers with stylish, sustainable clothing that reflects their individuality while promoting environmental consciousness. At Treeranga, we believe that fashion should not only look good but also feel good, knowing that each purchase contributes to a healthier planet.

At Treeranga, we are passionate about redefining the t-shirt experience. We combine creativity and sustainability to offer innovative designs that resonate with today's conscious consumer. With a focus on responsibly sourced materials and ethical production methods, we aim to inspire a new era of fashion that supports both personal expression and the well-being of our planet. Join us on our journey to make fashion more sustainable, one t-shirt at a time.

All our products are crafted with premium fabrics and meet the highest quality standards for comfort and style.

Treenga t-shirts are made from premium quality fabrics, primarily 100% organic cotton, ensuring breathability, comfort, and durability. We also offer blends such as cotton-polyester for a softer feel and extra flexibility. All materials are ethically sourced and eco-friendly.

You can place an order through our website by browsing our collections, selecting the t-shirts you like, and proceeding with secure checkout. We also offer bulk orders for businesses, which can be placed by contacting our customer service team.

Yes, we provide home delivery across India and internationally. Delivery times and fees vary based on location, and we offer free shipping on orders above a certain amount.

What payment methods does Treenga accept?
We accept multiple payment methods including credit/debit cards, UPI, PayPal, and cash on delivery for select locations.
