Return Policy

At Niwa Agro, we strive to provide the highest quality products and services to our customers. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we are here to help.

  • Return Eligibility :
    • Items must be returned within 30 days from the date of purchase.
    • Items must be unused, in the same condition that you received them, and in their original packaging.
    • Proof of purchase, such as a receipt or order confirmation, is required for all returns.
  • Non-Returnable Items :
      Certain items are not eligible for return, including:
    • Perishable goods (e.g., seeds, live plants)
    • Custom or personalized orders
    • Items marked as final sale
  • Return Process :
      To initiate a return, please follow these steps:
    • Contact our customer service team at [email address] or [phone number] to request a return authorization.
    • Once your return is authorized, package the item securely and include your proof of purchase.
    • Ship the item to the following address: Niwa Agro Returns [Address] [City, State, Zip Code]
  • Refunds :
    • Once we receive your return, we will inspect the item and notify you of the status of your refund.
    • If your return is approved, we will process a refund to your original method of payment within 10 business days.
    • Shipping costs are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, the cost of return shipping will be deducted from your refund.
  • Exchanges :
    • We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange an item, contact us at [email address] or [phone number].
  • Shipping Returns :
    • You will be responsible for paying for your own shipping costs for returning your item. Shipping costs are non-refundable.
    • We recommend using a trackable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance. We cannot guarantee that we will receive your returned item.
  • Contact Us :
      If you have any questions about our return policy, please contact us at:
    • Email: niwacare@outlook.com
